Gryffindor Slytherin Ravenclaw Hufflepuff

GRYFFINDOR

Those in this house are well known fo their bravery and courage. There house animal is the lion and their colors are scarlet and gold. Gryffindors never back down from a fight or doing what they believe is right.

SLYTHERIN

Slytherins tended to be ambitious, shrewd, cunning, strong leaders, and achievement oriented. They also had highly developed senses of self-preservation. This means that slytherins tended to hesitate before acting, so as to weigh all possible outcomes before deciding exacty what should be done.

RAVENCLAW

Ravenclaw was one of the four houses of Hogwarts School Of Witchcraft and Wizardy. Its Founder was the medieval witch Rowena Ravenclaw. Members of this house were characterized by their wit, learning and wisdom. The emblematic animal symbol was eagle, and blue and bronze were its colours.

HUFFLEPUFF

Hufflepuff refers to one of four Hogwarts houses in the Harry Potter, they known for having members that are patient, fair, hard working, and blandly nice.
